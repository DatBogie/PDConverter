About This Site

This site is a tool used to convert ADOFAI levels to Planets Dance levels.
It provides the level's data to copy/paste into Planets Dance, and also can
upload the song and any other image assets to Roblox*.

* PD Converter stores none of the information (API key(s), image(s), level data, etc.) you upload to it. PD Converter will upload all images referenced in the uploaded .ADOFAI fileâ€”make sure no innapropriate assets are used, otherwise your Roblox account may recieve punishment from Roblox.
